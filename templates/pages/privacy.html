Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

Veloria collects the minimum data necessary to operate the service. When you perform a search, the query and results are stored so they can be shared and revisited. Searches marked as private are visible only to those with the URL. Searches made when logged in are linked to your user.

Authentication

If you sign in via a third-party provider (GitHub, GitLab, or Atlassian), Veloria stores your display name and avatar URL solely to personalise your session. We do not access your repositories, email address, or any other account data beyond what is needed for login.

Cookies

Veloria uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No analytics trackers, advertising scripts, or third-party cookies are loaded.

Server Logs

Standard server logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) are retained for a limited period for debugging and abuse prevention, then deleted.

Third Parties

Veloria does not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. The only external services involved are the OAuth providers you choose to sign in with.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
